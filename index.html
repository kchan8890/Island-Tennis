Meet the Founder

The story behind Island Tennis

Island Tennis started on a single public court with one belief: every player has a fiercer game inside them waiting to break out. We coach for that moment — when technique, mindset, and momentum collide and you simply crush it.

Our mascot, the sea turtle, says everything about how we coach. Calm under pressure. Relentless over the long haul. Powerful when it matters most. We build players who last — not just for one match, but for a lifetime of the game. That same spirit lives in Crush, our court-ready apparel line.

  • USPTA-certified coaching philosophy
  • Personalized development plans for every player
  • Junior, adult, and competitive programs
